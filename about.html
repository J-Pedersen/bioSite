Terri's Biography

About Terri

Terri was born in June of 1975 in Colorado Springs, CO in the shadow of the Rocky Mountains. She has one sister and is the younger sibling of the two girls. After a brief time in Germany, her mother, sister and Terri came back to the United States here they settled in southeastern Wisconsin where she grew up. As Terri got older, she found that she had a passion for helping others and decided to pursue a career in psychology.

After many years of hard work and determination, she became a licensed addiction counselor, working with individuals struggling with substance abuse and addiction. Her work with addicts has been both rewarding and challenging, but she takes pride in the difference she has been able to make in the lives of others who need help.

When Terri isn't working she loves to relax and unwind. She loves gaming and spends hours each day playing her favorite games on her phone. Reading and writing have also been long-time hobbies of hers, she enjoys immersing herself in a good book or putting pen to paper and she one day hopes to be a published author. Terri is also a fan of movies and television and watches a wide variety of genres, from classic films to true crime dramas. Baking and shopping are two other activities that Terri enjoys and she loves trying out new recipes and experimenting with different ingredients. She also finds shopping to be a stress-reliever and enjoys treating herself to new clothes and accessories.

Terri considers learning to drive one of her most challenging accomplishments to date. Despite her initial nerves, she persevered and was eventually able to pass her driving test after thirty years, something that she had never thought possible. She is incredibly proud of herself and considers it one of her greatest achievements.

She is the mother of two beautiful children, and they are the source of her greatest pride. She loves them more than anything in the world and enjoys spending time with them, creating new memories and watching how they have grown into adults. Her best friends describe her as goofy or moody, and she has a unique outlook on life. Terri describes herself as an optimistic pessimist, she tries to see the best in every situation but is realistic about the challenges she might face in life.

She values trust in her relationships and she believes it is the key to her success in most aspects of life including relationships, parenting, and maintaining a happy marriage. When Terri is feeling stressed or disappointed she takes some time for herself to just be alone. She finds solace in playing games or meditating. She believes that hard work and honesty are the keys to a successful career, and she is constantly working to improve herself and her skills.

Terri's family is what makes her feel the happiest and most fulfilled. She greatly appreciates all the love and support that her friends and family have given her and she knows that they will always be there for her. She believes that contentment is the key to a successful life and is dedicated to finding happiness and peace and fun in every aspect of her life.
