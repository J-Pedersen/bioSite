Terri's Biography

Terri's Hobbies

Games

Terri loves to play games on her phone. She enjoys the challenge of solving puzzles and finding hidden words, and the satisfaction she feels when she finally succeeds. One ofthe things she loves most about these games is that they exercise her brain and keep her mind sharp.

Additionally Terri finds that playing her games is a good way for her to decompress and unwind after her long and stressful day. Puzzles and word games are amoung her favorites but solitaire is a game that she also plays quite often.

Reading

Reading is a pass time Terri enjoys but doesn't do as much as she used to because other things seem more important at the time or life gets in the way. From a young age, she was drawn to books and reading about all the wonderous worlds created by her favorite writers.

Terri favorite genres are mystery and horror novels but she also enjoys crime dramas and non-fiction as well.

Movies

Movies and tv shows take up a large portion of Terri's free time. Quite often she will find a show that she gets into and just binge watches the entire series over the course of a week or two.She likes all things zombie or crime drama or comedy.

A couple of her favorite movies and tv shows are "The Princess Bride" and "The Walking Dead". Some of Terri's best times have been time spent watching movies or tv shows with her family and friends.
