Terri's Dreams

Writing

Being a published Author is one of Terri"s biggest goals in life and she has some great ideas of what to write about but she just needs the time and motivation to make that happen so this is a goal I fully expect her to reach.

Terri has been passionate about writing since she was a young girl, and has always had a natural talent for storytelling. Terri envisions herself as a published author, sharing her stories with the world and inspiring others to follow their dreams. She is determined to make this a reality, and is excited to see where her writing journey will take her.

Travel

Visting Tibet is high on Terri's bucketlist. She hopes to be able to travel there and visit some of the buddist monastaries and just soak in some of the beauty and tranquillity of being in the mountains.

Terri has always been fascinated by the teachings of Buddhism and would love to learn more about the religion while in Tibet. She believes that immersing herself in the culture and daily practices of the monks would be a life-changing experience. Additionally, she is excited to explore the breathtaking landscapes and scenery that Tibet has to offer.

Academia

Terri would like to eventually go back to college for her masters degree or perhaps even a doctorate. She also has considered becoming a professor and teaching psychology and or substance abuse counseling to others. Perhaps even starting her own clinic.

In the meantime, Terri is focused on building her career in the field of psychology. She has been working as a therapist for the past few years and has recently considered taking on a supervisory role at work. Terri believes that continuing education is key to advancing her career and achieving her long-term goals.
